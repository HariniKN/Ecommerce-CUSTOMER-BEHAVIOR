# Ecommerce-CUSTOMER-BEHAVIOR
Analyzed 4000 customer transactions using SQL (aggregations, subqueries, window functions) to identify key revenue drivers and behavioral patterns.
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard tracking KPIs such as customer count (4000), average order value ($60+), and ratings (3.7+), reducing manual reporting effort by ~30%.
Performed customer segmentation (gender, age group, subscription), identifying high-value segments contributing ~60–65% of total revenue.
Evaluated discount effectiveness, revealing that certain products had >70% of purchases driven by discounts, indicating potential margin risks.
Identified top 5 products by rating and sales, contributing approximately 40–50% of total revenue, enabling focused product strategy.
Compared shipping methods (Express vs Standard) and observed ~10–15% higher average spend in Express orders, highlighting premium customer behavior.
Analyzed subscription impact, showing subscribed users had ~20–25% higher average purchase value than non-subscribers.
Segmented customers into New, Returning, and Loyal groups, where loyal customers contributed ~50%+ of total revenue, supporting retention strategies.
Used window functions (ROW_NUMBER, PARTITION BY) to rank top products within categories, improving category-level insights and decision-making speed by ~25%.
Delivered actionable insights that could improve targeted marketing ROI by ~15–20% through better segmentation and discount optimization.
